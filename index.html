Explosive growth in Chinese travellers

According to the Financial Times, the income of the top 20% Chinese population has grown 500% over the past ten years.


Chinese travellers are sophisticated and have more money to spend.


The standard tourist sites are no longer attractive to many Chinese. Instead, they are looking for the unique travel experience - one that they can share among their friends.


Wechat is the platform that the Chinese use to share their life experiences.






Can you offer something unique?

Whether you are an owner of a boutique hotel in the Maldives, operate a diving boat in Lombok, or run a safari in Kenya. If you want to reach out to the Chinese traveller, Wechat is the perfect platform.


There are 300 million Wechat users in China alone. We operate a Wechat travel portal. Wechat users find out about travel experiences through this portal.


They also use the Friend's Circle to share their experiences about their travel. Wechat is a very powerful medium to reach these travellers.
